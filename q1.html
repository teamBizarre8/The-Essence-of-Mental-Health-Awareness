    Need of viewers

    The needs of the viewers while streaming podcasts about the Essence of Mental Health Awareness to Junior High School Students is; the audience needs to learn what are the factors that affect Mental Health, as well as its effects to a person. They are also in need to differentiate the difference between the mental disorders.

    Characteristics of a good stream

    In streaming, the camera angle has to be good, there should be no background noises and the internet connection should be stable even if the stream is not gaming. Aside from those, being talkative is also important. While streaming you should interact with the audience. While streaming, there should be no one disrupting the streamer because it will make the audience confused and it will divert their attention. A good stream should also be entertaining and informative to the viewers.
