Goal/Purpose:

The purpose of our group's topic is to tackle mental health, its elements, and why it should be discussed. The majority of the people we know either struggle with something or have bad mental health, which is something our group is aware of. We want to help folks who struggle with communication and problem-solving on their own. By spreading why it is importance to spread awareness and information about Mental Health, we can make a great difference.

About Us

We are Group 8 of A01-10-S2-1 from APEC Schools V. Luna. Our stream talks about the Essence of Mental Health Awareness to Junior High School Students. We focus on giving detailed information about the causes and effects of poor mental health, tips on how to prevent having poor mental health, and how we can help those who suffer from mental illness.
